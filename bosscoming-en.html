BossComing Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2024

BossComing is developed by Yaoguang Du (“we”, “our”, “us”). We respect your privacy and explain below how the app handles data.

Information We Collect

  • BossComing does not collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information.
  • The app only accesses on-device motion sensors (gyroscope, accelerometer) to detect flip/shake gestures; this data never leaves your device.
  • No accounts, cloud services, contacts, photos, location, or other sensitive data are accessed.

In-App Purchases

BossComing Pro transactions are processed solely by Apple via App Store. We never see your payment details. Purchase status is saved locally to unlock features.

Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or rent any data to third parties. The app contains no advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

BossComing is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Data Security

All preferences (e.g., unlocked status, trigger settings) stay on device using Apple’s secure storage.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new effective date here and mention significant changes in release notes when relevant.
