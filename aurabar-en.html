AuraBar Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

1. Data We Collect

AuraBar does not collect personally identifiable information. The app only stores the mood text, stickers, background colors, and similar preferences you choose. These settings are kept locally on your device and synchronized between the main app and its widgets/Live Activity via Apple’s App Groups. Purchase status is validated by Apple’s StoreKit receipts; this information stays on your device and Apple’s servers and is never uploaded to third-party services.

2. How We Use and Share Data

All settings data is used solely to render the app, widgets, and Live Activity experiences. We do not share any information with third parties. AuraBar does not embed third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

3. Third-Party Services

The app relies on Apple frameworks such as StoreKit, ActivityKit, and WidgetKit, and adheres to Apple’s privacy policies. Purchases, downloads, and refunds made through the App Store are processed directly by Apple; we never receive your payment details.

4. Data Security and Retention

All configuration data remains on your device. You can erase it by deleting the app or using the in-app “Reset” option. Sandbox or production purchase receipts are stored by Apple and can be restored at any time through “Restore Purchases.”

5. Children and Age Restrictions

AuraBar is rated 4+ and does not collect personal data that could identify children.

6. Contact Us

If you have any privacy-related questions, contact us at yaogdu@gmail.com.
